# Noctari

Noctari are shadow-touched humanoids whose forms seem to blur at the edges, slipping easily between light and absence.

## Features

### Shadow Slip

When you move through darkness, cover, or obscured spaces, you can mark a Stress to move to a location within Far range.

### Fade from Sight

Once per long rest, you can become Hidden until you make your next attack or take a noticeable action. Attacks made while Hidden from this feature have advantage.

## Design notes

This ancestry draws inspiration from _fetchling-like_ concepts found in other systems, which often rely on vision-based mechanics. Since Daggerheart doesn’t emphasize those rules, this design shifts toward movement, positioning, and a more abstract connection to shadows.
