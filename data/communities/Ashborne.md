# Ashborne

Being part of a ashborne community means you were shaped by volatile landscapes, where the ground shifts, danger is constant, and survival depends on acting without hesitation.

## Features

### Instinctive Reflex

Once per session, after making a reaction roll, you can mark a Stress to reroll it.

## Design notes

The idea here is simple: someone who lives in extremely dangerous, unstable environments and has developed fast reflexes in order to survive.
