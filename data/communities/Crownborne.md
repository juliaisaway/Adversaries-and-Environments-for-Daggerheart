# Crownborne

Being part of a crownborne community means you were shaped by sprawling cities, where towering structures, endless crowds, and shifting power define the rhythm of life.

## Features

### Crowdsense

You have advantage on rolls to navigate crowded environments, find efficient routes, and move through dense groups of people.

## Design notes

I felt there was a gap for a Community that sits adjacent to Heartborne. While there are several Communities focused on small, tight-knit places, there wasn’t one that represented life in a large city. Highborne leans into nobility and status, but doesn’t capture the experience of everyday people navigating an urban environment. Crownborne is meant to fill that space.
