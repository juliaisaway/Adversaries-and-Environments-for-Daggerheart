# Stageborne

Being part of a stageborne community means you were raised among performers, where identity is fluid and life is shaped by constant performance and adaptation.

## Features

### Perfect Act

You have advantage on rolls to assume a role, maintain a persona, or control your expression under pressure.

## Design notes

I wanted to avoid something too Bard-esque, so instead I focused on the lived experience of performers, especially those in traveling troupes or circuses. The goal was to capture a lifestyle built around performance, adaptation, and constantly stepping into different roles, rather than just artistic skill.
