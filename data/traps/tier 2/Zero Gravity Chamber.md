# Zero Gravity Chamber

A concealed mechanism destabilizes gravity within the room, sending everything into a slow, disorienting drift.

## Purpose

Disrupt positioning, disorient targets, create tension through unstable movement

## Features

### Gravity Nullification

#### Trigger

When a target enters the room.

#### Effect

The gravity within the room collapses, causing all creatures and loose objects to float. When a PC attempts to move, they must make an Agility Reaction Roll. On a success, they may move up to Very Close range. On a failure, they drift uncontrollably and remain in place.

### Gravity Surge

#### Trigger

When the mechanism is deactivated.

#### Effect

Gravity violently returns to normal. All targets still within the room must take 1d10+3 physical damage.

## Design notes

A different concept of a room that disrupt movement. The mechanism controlling the chamber is located elsewhere, so the players must find the mechanism in order to progress within the chamber.
